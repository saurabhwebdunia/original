Media - Intellek CMS

Over the time thoughts are mounting, so does technology. These days people are highly tech savvy and consider Websites and Mobile Phones as major source of getting update. Thus, it is crucial for information sources to be updated, organized and maintained every time. This real time need gives birth to Content Management System (CMS).

How you visualize the CMS? Can we assist you to augment it?

CMS could simply consider as a system that organizes & manages content. However, it is precisely the obviousness of what a CMS is and does. Such a vague statement; created a degree of confusion amongst 'would be' purchasers of such a system. And apparently cover up all kinds of solutions which a content management system offers.

Eventually, there is ongoing interest in the pace of CMS due to its influence on easy management of content, its accessibility, integration, re-purposing & publishing. Thereby CMS becomes the ultimate key to unleash success of your content.

Webdunia’s media solution, ‘Intellek CMS’ is a broad client windows, for any non-tech savvy person, based desktop application supported by assorted web based applications which can be used for publishing web content consisting of text, images, audio, video etc. on the website and mobile.

  • User friendly – Easy to use
  • Multilingual solution with Unicode support
  • Template based HTML generation ensuring consistent design
  • Role based security
  • URL scanner tool to avoid malicious URLs
  • Auto upload provision
  • Archived content managed through web based module
  • RSS feeds
