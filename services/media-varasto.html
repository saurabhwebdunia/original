Media Varasto

Rich media content is the essence of any websites as it gives profundity to it. Whilst it’s quite time consuming and haphazard process to search for relevant media content from Internet, especially when you require them for real time usage.

Why to invest time and efforts for browsing pertinent images, videos or sounds? Managing the media content now won’t be that complicated with Media Varasto.

You’ve got the great product and so you’re developing a great website. Media Varasto is a repository system that allows you storing & managing humongous media content. It enables you to access variety of primary source materials including imagery, videos, and audios. Files can be stored and employed including resizing, cropping and format conversion. Additionally you can categorize your search by using Media Varasto’s unique search feature and upload an image, an audio and a video on the website.

Benefits offer:

  • Managing humungous content becomes very easy
  • Insert and resize images easily
  • Image editor is browser based
  • Easily insert movies and other video content
  • Various formats in which gallery can be displayed on website
