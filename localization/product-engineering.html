Product Engineering

Localization Engineering is the mainstay of any localization process. Engineering is interwoven into the localization process right from file analysis and preparation for localization to checking files with the in-build tool functionality and custom made internal tools. It also takes care of any font or other issues which may reduce quality of localized builds. Engineers also look into version controlling in case of long-term projects and bug-fixing is one of their major responsibilities

At Webdunia, we have experienced engineers as well as teams that are specially trained to work with a number of software tools, utilities and platforms with a process-oriented methodology. Our localization engineering capabilities are proven in improving the consistency, quality, support to the client and reducing overall project costs.
