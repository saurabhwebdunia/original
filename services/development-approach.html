Development Approach

We follow agile methodologies for project development. Amongst all the agile methodologies, Webdunia uses Scrum as it helps to track releases as per the real world progress of any project. We adopt 2 â€“ 3 weeks of sprint to ensure that project direction is adjusted or reoriented based on the completed work, thus avoiding speculations and project variances.

Our philosophy for project development is a 2 way structured approach:

  • Project Planning
  • Project Execution
  • Project Management

Project Planning

Our Project Plan is the outcome of various processes and best practices. It involves creating of a set of plans to help our project execution team through execution and closure phases of the project. The plans created helps to manage time, cost, quality, change, risk and issues. Also useful to deal with employees and clients to ensure to deliver the project on time and within budget. Project Development Lifecycle:
Project will be developed using the Waterfall lifecycle/methodology where:-

  • Work is done in stages
  • Reviews are conducted between stages
  • Reviews represent quality gates and decision points for continuing
  • Orderly sequence of development steps to ensure the adequacy of project documentation and design reviews to provide the quality, reliability and maintainability of the developed system
